Award Definitions

What each award means

Each category recognises a specific aspect of performance and contribution across the season.

Batting Average
Highest average runs per innings across the season.
Batting Aggregate
Most total runs scored across all innings.
Bowling Average
Lowest runs conceded per wicket taken.
Bowling Aggregate
Most wickets taken across the season.
Club Champion
Best overall performer across batting, bowling & fielding.
Most Improved
Player showing the greatest growth during the season.
Best Clubman
Recognising dedication, team spirit, and off-field contribution.
Fielding Award
Outstanding contribution in the field across the season.
